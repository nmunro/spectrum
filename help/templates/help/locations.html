Help: Locations

Locations are a required part of events, locations will inform people where a given event will take place. An organisation may have many locations, and may allocate different locations to different events.

Before creating an event, an organisation will need to have at least one location saved.

From the interface below, you will be able to view, add, edit, and delete locations from an organisation.
