Help: Organisations

Organisations are a way to arrange related events and resources that people may attend or take advantage of.

Organisations are a special type of object in Spectrum, users do not have the permission to create or delete organisations. If an organisation needs to be created or deleted, a Spectrum administrator must be contacted.

It is possible to edit an organisation.
