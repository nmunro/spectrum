Help

Selby Town Primary Care Network organised a meeting in July 2023 with a view to bringing interested stakeholders together to help to promote awareness of neurodiversity in the Selby District. They had previously carried out a survey on existing provision and discovered that were was very little provision for in the district for people living with neurodiversity. The group aims to provide help to organisations / groups looking to set up events and resources that will help people living with neurodiversity and as part of this provision were keen to produce a directory of neurodiversity provision in the district.

A local charity called Spirit of Christmas offered to put a directory together of existing provision in the district from information provided by other organisations attending the meeting. However, the charity had a volunteer who specialises in code writing for computer programmes and this volunteer put together a small team who would develop an online resource where individuals could search for resources / events in their locality and where groups / organisations could promote what they do. If you are reading this introduction, then you are looking to promote resources or activities that will be of interest to people living with neurodiversity.

Thank you for signing up to promote what you do. It is hoped that as the site grows it will help to provide the general public with details of what is happening in their locality with regards to neurodivergent provision.
