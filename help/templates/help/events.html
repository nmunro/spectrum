Help: Events

Events are a type of object that an organisation may make available. An event is, as it sounds, an event that members of the public may attend. It may be ticketed or not, and may be marked as such to inform people to ensure they have tickets prior to attending.

From the interface below, you will be able to view, add, edit, and delete events from an organisation.
