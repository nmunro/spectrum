Help: Contacts

Contacts are a required part of events, contacts will be used for people to reach out to a designated individual responsible for managing the event in question. An organisation may have many contacts, and may allocate different contacts to different events.

Before creating an event, an organisation will need to have at least one contact saved.

From the interface below, you will be able to view, add, edit, and delete contacts from an organisation.
